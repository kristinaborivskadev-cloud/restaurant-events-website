Start your career at Bartolo

We’re always on the lookout for passionate and dedicated individuals to join the Bartolo family. Our team thrives on enthusiasm, creativity, and a love for exceptional hospitality. If you’re eager to bring your unique skills, energy, and expertise to a dynamic and fast-paced environment, we’d be thrilled to meet you. We offer exciting opportunities for personal and professional growth, as well as a supportive workplace where your contributions truly matter. Explore our open positions today and take the first step toward an exciting and fulfilling career with us!

Waiter / Waitress (Server)

We are looking for an energetic and attentive server to help our guests enjoy every moment at Bartolo.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience in the restaurant industry is a plus
  • Excellent communication skills with guests
  • Responsible, detail-oriented, and organized
  • Knowledge of English is an advantage

Benefits:

  • Work in a stable and friendly team
  • Competitive salary + tips
  • Free meals during shifts
  • Opportunities for career growth and professional development

Working Hours:

  • Flexible schedule with the option to choose (day/evening)
  • Weekend and holiday shifts according to schedule
